Things to know about football

Names of different skills in football

  1. The Rabona
  2. The Knuckleball
  3. Bicycle kick
  4. Curling

    5. In association football, the rabona is the technique of kicking the football where the kicking leg is crossed behind the back of the standing leg.The knuckleball in football refers to a ball kicked at very low spin, which results in a zigzag trajectory.In association football, a bicycle kick, also known as an overhead kick or scissors kick, is an acrobatic strike where a player kicks an airborne ball rearward in midair.Curl or bend in association football is a definition for a spin on the ball which makes the ball move in a curved direction.
